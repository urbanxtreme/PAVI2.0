Water Conservation Tips

Shower Smart

Take shorter showers and install a water-efficient showerhead to save up to 15 liters per minute.

Fix Leaks

A dripping faucet can waste more than 11,000 liters of water per year. Fix leaks promptly!

Smart Gardening

Water plants early morning or late evening to reduce evaporation. Use mulch to retain moisture.

Efficient Appliances

Use water-efficient washing machines and dishwashers. Run them only when full.
